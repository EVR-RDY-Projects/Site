High Cost Solutions

Our Products

T.A.P.S.

Telemetry Acquisition Platform Solution

A suite of open‑source tools being developed to create low‑cost, practical solutions that move critical telemetry from the OT environment into the IT environment for safe ingestion into a SIEM.

SEER Sensor

PoC

Scalable Endpoint & Environmental Reconnaissance

Provides low‑cost visibility for OT/ICS and IT networks by capturing mirrored traffic, generating Zeek‑style logs, ingesting JSON from endpoints, and storing forensic data locally.

Why it matters: Smaller utilities and DoD‑critical infrastructure sites gain OT visibility without enterprise‑level licensing costs.

GHOST Node

PoC

Hardware‑Enforced Data Diode

A hardware‑enforced data diode for unidirectional UDP flow, built from inexpensive media converters, switches, and fiber.

Why it matters: Provides a physical security guarantee that data flows only one way — safeguarding OT environments while enabling centralized monitoring.

RAMPART

Active Development

Resilient Architecture for Monitoring & Proactive Advanced Response Teams

Bridges the GHOST Diode and the SIEM of choice (currently Google Chronicle). Packages data for SIEM and allows remote PCAP analysis from SEER without breaking OT isolation.

Why it matters: Ensures telemetry can be safely centralized, normalized, and analyzed for proactive monitoring and forensics.

TacSOC

Tactical Security Operations Center

EVR:RDY's first flagship product: an AI-enforced, OT-focused SOC built for precision threat hunting, incident response, and real defense. Where traditional SOCs drown in alerts, TacSOC runs lean.

AI Integration

Uses advanced AI models to deduplicate and enrich telemetry, map events to MITRE ATT&CK, and automatically dismiss noise.

Result: Analysts focus on true threats, enabling faster detection and resilient defenses.

Resource Pooling

Leverages cloud‑native SIEMs — currently Google Chronicle — to enable license sharing across customers.

Result: Provides enterprise‑grade monitoring without enterprise‑grade costs.

Hunter/Killer Teams

Small, skilled groups blending Tier‑2 and Tier‑3 analysts under a Team Leader. AI takes Tier-1 work off the table, accelerating progression and scaling defense to many sites.

Result: TacSOC is both a defense platform and a training engine.

R.A.I.D. Framework

Recon, Acquire, Interpret, Defend — a systematic approach to threat hunting and defense operations.

Result: Enables small, distributed teams to operate with autonomy and accountability without massive centralized SOCs.
