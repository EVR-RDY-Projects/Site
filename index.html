EVR:RDY is a veteran-led initiative focused on bringing affordable and effective cybersecurity to critical infrastructure and DoD-adjacent organizations.

Our mission is to solve three major challenges:

  • High Cost — Providing affordable sensing and monitoring solutions where traditional tools are priced out of reach.
  • Workforce Gaps — Leveraging automation and veteran expertise to strengthen understaffed teams.
  • Scalability — Designing modular solutions that can adapt to utilities, SMBs, and critical infrastructure alike.

We are building a platform that combines low-cost OT/ICS sensors, unidirectional data diodes, and AI-enabled SOC automation to empower organizations to defend forward and advance always.
